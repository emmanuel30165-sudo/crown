About Us

Crown Paints, made simple to order

Crown Paints is a registered, authorized distributor of Crown Paints Kenya PLC products — making it easier to find the right colour and place your order by WhatsApp.

Who We Are

Crown Paints was created to make ordering paint simpler. We're a registered, authorized distributor of Crown Paints products, based in Nairobi County, and we serve customers across Kenya.

Every product we sell is sourced through official Crown Paints distribution channels. When you order from us, you're getting the products Crown manufactures, at the prices Crown sets.

✅ Registered Distributor 🎨 1,000+ Crown Colours 🚚 All 47 Counties

Why We Built It This Way

Most paint shopping in Kenya still means visiting a hardware store, hoping they have your colour in stock, and figuring out delivery yourself. We wanted something simpler — browse the full Crown colour range online, see pricing upfront, and order directly through WhatsApp without creating accounts or navigating a complicated checkout.

Our delivery network covers all 47 counties, with free delivery on orders above KES 10,000 — because where you live in Kenya shouldn't limit access to Crown Paints.
